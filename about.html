About Me.

I have always been told that one of my prevailing characteristics is my desire to explore and seek new experiences. And that is my philisophy: that there is always something more out there that I can reach for, and to never settle in place. This is because throughout my life I have discovered that there are so many aspects and shades of life. To only be able to live out a tiny fragment of what is possible in this world would be limiting yourself to the circumstances that you were given. I wish to see the world in unique perspectives and portray it to those I meet.

My name is Winggo Tse and I'm a fourth year computer science student at University of California, Santa Cruz. I decided to create this website not only to showcase my work but to also use it as a platform to reach out to others who wish to get to know more about me. I would love to listen to what you have to share, whether you share a similar interest or just felt inspired.

As a student about to finish his last year, much of my time this season is dedicated to exploring the many opportunities I have been blessed with. I treasure the opportunity to capture the valuable moments of those I have been blessed to meet and showcase the many perspectives of the world through photography. The rest of my time I devote into studying computer science and building meaningful software. I LOVE meeting people so please don't hesitate to reach out to me!

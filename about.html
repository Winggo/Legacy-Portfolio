Things about me

All my life, the idea of exploring, conquering the unknown, abosrbing new experiences has always been a driving factor in most that I do. My philisophy is that there is always something more out there that I can reach for, and I wouldn't ever want to settle in place. There are so many aspects and shades of life -- I wish to experience all of them.

I hope to use this site to document and share moments of my adventures. I want these pictures to be a genuine and raw window to times I have experienced. I wish to convey not only what appears through my eyes, but also share the excitement and drive I have for seeking what's out there.

Right now, I'm studying computer science my third year here at University of California, Santa Cruz. If you are ever in the area, feel free to leave a message. I would love to share and hear more. I've always wanted to combine computer science and photography --two things I see myself doing for the rest of my life-- to create something I'm proud of. And I hope this will always be on ongoing project of mine, to always seek to keep growing.
